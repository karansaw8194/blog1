About the Issue

The Kancha Gachibowli forest in Hyderabad is facing deforestation due to developmental activities. This threatens the natural habitat of many species and the green cover of the city.

Impact on Wildlife

Animals are losing their homes, and though viral videos show injured elephants, fact-checkers confirm they are not from Hyderabad. Still, the threat to wildlife is real.
